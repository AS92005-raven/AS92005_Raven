How can we fix this?

Deforestation

Government must enforce laws against illegal logging. We should also empower our Indigenous communities because they are the most affected if deforestation continuous to happen. We need to support national and global groups like the United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change so that we can restore degraded lands and plant more trees to reduce the carbon dioxide in our atmosphere so we can build a spiritual connection between the land and the people.

Waste Management

Kaitiakitanga means moving away from single-use plastics, and we also need to practise reuse, reduce and recycle so we can maximise the use of the plastic and not ending up in the ocean that kills our marine life. We can turn our food scraps into a compost that can be used and shows Kaitiakitanga. The food scraps that we turn into a compost, it can help our plants to be more healthy as it returns the nutrients to the soil and can regenerate the land.

Industrial emissions

We should replace the use of fuel and coal as it only contributes more carbon dioxide to the atmosphere to the use of renewable energy like solar panels, windmills, hydroelectric power and geothermal energy. With this kind of sources of energy, we are reducing the carbon dioxide to the atmosphere. Although this might be slower to produce energy unlike the fossil fuel and coal but this is a good alternative if we want to protect our land and for the people in the next generation.
