Deforestation

Understanding the impact of cutting trees in forests on local biodiversity.

Every tree lost destroys our planet's natural respiratory system. Kaitiakitanga calls us to restore these roots.

Participate to restore native tree species and bring back lost biodiversity.

Waste Management

Implementing effective organic, paper, and plastic separation strategies within local communities.

Reducing landfill reliance preserves soil health and protects freshwater systems from runoff pollution.

Practice conscious consumption like reduce, reuse, and recycle accurately every day.

Industrial Emissions

Monitoring air quality and pushing for green manufacturing infrastructures to reduce carbon footprints.

Smog and greenhouse gases directly compromise human health and accelerate global climate disruption.

Using renewable energy like solar panels and hydro systems to produce energy and to promote Kaitiakitanga that protects our atmosphere.
